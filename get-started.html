How to use kostro ?

First, you have to prepare a list of schools that you want to get some informations about them.

You need to have their website domain (like mit.edu).

Then, go to kostro

Go to kostro and in the field, put the first website domain, then, click "Add", ...

Click on "Submit"

Now you can navigate between the "profiles" of all the schools on your list and you can get their info (if there is no error :)).
