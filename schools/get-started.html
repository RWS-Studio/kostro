How to use kostro as a school ?

First, get the files

You can get the json files that you just have to put the values at the good places.

Next, get all your data

Try to fill the fields with your datas.
Warning: for config.json, in the "files" dict, if you don't want to put a file, where there is "[the_file_you_dont_want]": "on" you must replace "on" by "off", then, delete the [the_file_you_dont_want].json.
For updates.json, you need to put the current time at the format displayed (dd/mm/yyyy or precise format if different), and you have to put your name and a way to contact you (professionnal phone or email address).

After that, verify that all datas are correct and next publish the files on the web.

There is a configured url that kostro will try to get your data : http://[your_website]/kostro/config.json (so you need to put your files in a folder named "kostro" in your website file manager). Then, it will get what are the files you want that users access to it and then we will get the datas contained on each resource when user clicks.

When the files are online, you can test if its works.

You can verify if the process works at https://rws-studio.github.io/kostro/, then, you have to put your website domain in the field, you have to click on "Add" and then on "Submit". On the new webpage, you can click on the resource button that you want to get the data.

Finally, share that you configured your datas for kostro!

If you share on your socials medias that you did this, you permit to grow the community of people that use kostro (a way to have more popularity on the web & to have more students).
